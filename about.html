Our Mission
To craft genre-defining games and legendary worlds for all to share.
We want to redefine what games can be and how you experience them. We aim to balance the love and attention our craft demands while delivering at the speed players expect. Through unparalleled creativity and storytelling, we create immersive universes and iconic characters that are beloved across platforms, borders, backgrounds, and generations - only made possible by building a work environment that nurtures the artistry of game development and unleashes the aspirations of our people.
